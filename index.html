Welcome!

Hello there, friend!

First, let me say how much I admire anyone who takes the step to invest in their health and strength - it shows you’re committed to becoming the best version of yourself. There really is no secret formula here; our role as coaches and trainers is simply to share the time-tested methods that have helped countless people reach their goals, whether that’s shedding pounds or building muscle. By mastering the fundamentals together, you’ll build a foundation that carries you forward, no matter where you start.

I know everyone’s journey is unique - this plan has transformed the routines of many, but you’re the expert on your own body. If you’re already ahead of the curve, fantastic! If not, I’m here to guide you every step of the way. Your success is my success, so let’s dive in, learn from each other, and celebrate every win along the road.

Week 1

Schedule

  • Monday & Thursday: Upper Body
  • Tuesday & Friday: Lower Body + Core
  • Wednesday, Saturday & Sunday: Active Rest (light walking, stretching)

Monday & Thursday - Upper Body

ExerciseSets × RepsNotes
Push-ups (or Bench Press)3 × 8–12Full range of motion
Bent-over Dumbbell Row3 × 8–12Keep back flat
Dumbbell Shoulder Press3 × 10Control weight
Lat Pull-down (or Pull-up)3 × 8–12Use assistance if needed
Biceps Curl2 × 12–15Lower slowly
Triceps Rope Push-down2 × 12–15Contract fully

Tuesday & Friday - Lower Body + Core

ExerciseSets × RepsNotes
Goblet Squat3 × 8–12Sit back into hips
Romanian Deadlift3 × 8–12Hinge at hips
Walking Lunges2 × 10 per legDrive through heel
Calf Raises3 × 15Full stretch
Plank3 × 30–45 secKeep body straight
Hanging Knee Raises3 × 10–15Controlled descent

Nutrition & Habits

  • Protein: ~1.8 g per kg bodyweight (~170 g/day)
  • Veggies & Fiber: 3 servings daily
  • Hydration: 3 L water daily
  • Sleep: 7–8 hours nightly
